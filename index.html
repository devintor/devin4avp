The Mission
Keeping it R.E.A.L.
Alpha is worth fighting for.
That's why I'm running.

Ten years from now, I want to be certain that Alpha is still something I'm proud to be a part of. Right now, I'm not sure we're on that path. We are becoming increasingly disconnected from one another. We're not being as truly impactful as we can be. We're misprioritizing our efforts. And we're failing to make effective use of our resources at a time when our pipelines and institutional equity efforts are under attack or have already been dismantled.

R.E.A.L. is a direct challenge to performative Alpha culture — transactional relationships, surface-level presence, conversations that stay at the surface and don't translate into change, and a network we're not using for each other. Every pillar is a call to be authentic in all that we do.
