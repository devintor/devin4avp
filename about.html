Who I Am
Alpha Delta Chapter · USC Class of 2027

I'm a junior at USC studying Industrial and Systems Engineering. I hold four leadership positions in Alpha Delta Chapter and serve as the Southern California Assistant District Director.

I am the type of person that when I think something needs to get done, I can't wait around for someone else to do it. Right now, I think Alpha West needs to break out of our comfort zone and reckon honestly with the direction we're heading. There are real problems in front of us, and we've been recycling surface-level solutions to them for too long.

I am incredibly pensive, highly strategic, and creative. I know how to build things, sustain them, and make them matter. I've done it in Alpha and outside of it. That's why I'm running — not because I think leadership is a title worth having, but because I think there's work that needs to be done and I can contribute meaningfully to it.
